# Product Recommendations — Revolut CX & Retention

## 1) Verification & Account Access
Problem: Verification loops and account restrictions drive extreme dissatisfaction.

Actions:
- Clear verification status timeline
- Reduce unnecessary re-verification triggers
- Transparent restriction reasons
- Defined SLAs for account unlock

---

## 2) Transfer Reliability
Problem: Pending or failed transfers create perceived financial risk.

Actions:
- Transparent state machine (submitted → processing → sent)
- Proactive delay notifications
- Clear resolution timelines

---

## 3) Support Prioritization
Problem: Slow response amplifies churn risk.

Actions:
- Sentiment-based ticket triage
- Priority routing for “money at risk” cases
- Measure FRT and resolution time by category

---

## 4) Card Stability & Disputes
Problem: Card declines and dispute confusion impact trust.

Actions:
- Better decline reason clarity
- Transparent dispute workflow
- Fast escalation path

---

## 5) Predictive Monitoring Layer
Use churn proxy + sentiment to:
- Track weekly churn risk %
- Monitor theme frequency
- Trigger alerts when negative clusters spike
